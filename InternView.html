🚀 InternView

Many students can't afford costly learning. This platform helps you gain experience and skills for free!

Hello Friends!

Technology is moving fast. Skills matter more than ever. The earlier you start, the better your future.

As a student from a middle-class background I’ve faced the challenge of finding internships that are both accessible and affordable. Many opportunities either required payment or lacked proper guidance, leaving me unsure of how to prepare for my career. InternView was born from my own struggle a platform built to help students like me find unpaid internships, learn relevant skills, and prepare for placements without spending a single rupee. This project is more than code it’s my contribution to making internship opportunities free, fair, and accessible for everyone. If this helps even one student get closer to their dream career, I’ll consider it a success.

📘 What Are Unpaid Internships?

Unpaid internships offer hands-on experience without monetary compensation. They let students build portfolios and connect with professionals.

💡 Why They Matter

  • Work with professional tools and teams
  • Build confidence through real-world tasks
  • Strengthen your resume and career path
  • Access roles even with limited resources

🌱 Who Offers Them?

  • Startups: Tech, design, digital marketing
  • NGOs: Volunteering, outreach, media
  • Research Labs: Data, AI, social sciences
  • Education Platforms: Teaching and curriculum support

🏆 Advantages

  • Learn online at your place
  • No strict deadlines (depends, but mostly true)
  • Build professional connections
  • Gain skills not taught in college
  • Enhance your resume
  • Explore different roles and industries

📌 Summary

Unpaid internships give students valuable experience, skill growth, and exposure — all without pay. They bridge the gap between classroom learning and practical work. Even with financial constraints, these opportunities improve resumes, professional connections, and career clarity. Many organizations provide mentorship, certifications, and sometimes job recommendations.
